Discovery

The time invested in user research, business cases, and problem definition

Goals

I need to answer these questions before I feel comfortable offering solutions:

  • Who is the user?
  • Where are they in their journey?
  • What are the use cases?
  • What’s the user’s environment?
  • What’s the frequency and severity of the problem?
  • What’s the impact on business?
  • What are the success metrics?
